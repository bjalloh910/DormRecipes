Yummy Breakfast-Burritos

Ingredients

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice (white or brown)
  • 1/2 cup cooked and seasoned black beans (or your favorite beans)
  • 1/2 cup cooked and seasoned shredded chicken (or your preferred protein)
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or any cheese of your choice)
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • Sour cream and/or guacamole (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. Reheat your air fryer to 350째F (180째C) for a few minutes.
  2. Lay out the flour tortilla on a clean surface or a plate.
  3. In the center of the tortilla, add the cooked rice, seasoned black beans, shredded chicken, diced tomatoes, onions, and bell peppers.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the fillings.
  5. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and then roll it up tightly to form the burrito.
  6. Place the burrito seam side down in the air fryer basket.
  7. Air fry the burrito at 350째F (180째C) for about 6-8 minutes or until the tortilla becomes crispy and lightly browned. You may need to adjust the cooking time based on the size and power of your air fryer.
  8. Carefully remove the burrito from the air fryer and let it cool for a moment.
  9. Cut the burrito in half diagonally, if desired, for easier eating.
  10. Serve the air fryer burrito with salsa on the side, and if you like, top it with sour cream and guacamole.

Enjoy your delicious and crispy air fryer burrito! You can customize the fillings to suit your taste preferences, adding ingredients like avocado, lettuce, or different types of cheese.
