Pancake Pop-Pops: Bite-Sized Fluffy Delights!

Ingredients

    You only need TWO ingredients!!

  • Frozen pancakes
  • Cooking spray or oil (for greasing the air fryer basket)

Steps

  1. Preheat the Air Fryer: Preheat your air fryer to around 350°F (175°C) for a few minutes.
  2. Prepare the Pancakes: Take the desired number of frozen pancakes from the packaging. You can usually place them in a single layer without overlapping.
  3. Grease the Air Fryer Basket: Lightly grease the air fryer basket with cooking spray or a small amount of oil. This will prevent the pancakes from sticking to the basket.
  4. Arrange the Pancakes: Place the frozen pancakes in a single layer in the greased air fryer basket. Make sure to leave some space between the pancakes to allow for proper air circulation.
  5. Cooking Time: The cooking time can vary depending on the thickness of the pancakes and the specific air fryer model. Generally, you'll want to cook the pancakes for about 5-8 minutes. However, it's a good idea to start with the lower end of the cooking time range and check on the pancakes to avoid overcooking.
  6. Check for Doneness: After the initial cooking time, open the air fryer and check the pancakes. They should be golden brown and heated through. If needed, you can cook them for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  7. Serve: Once the pancakes are cooked to your desired level of crispiness and warmth, carefully remove them from the air fryer using tongs or a spatula.
  8. Optional Toppings: Serve the air-fried pancakes with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, butter, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or even a dusting of powdered sugar.
  9. Enjoy: Now you can enjoy your delicious and convenient air-fried frozen pancakes!

This is such an easy and quick recipe. Enjoy your fluffy and delicious pancakes made in the air fryer! Feel free to add your own toppings.
