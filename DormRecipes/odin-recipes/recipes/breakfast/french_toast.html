Joyful French Toast Delight: A Recipe to Savor

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or your preferred type)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk (whole milk or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Butter or cooking spray (for greasing the air fryer basket)
  • Maple syrup, powdered sugar, or your favorite toppings (for serving)

Steps

  1. In a shallow dish or bowl, whisk together the egss, milk, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon until well combined.
  2. Preheat your air fryer to 375F(190C) for a few minutes.
  3. Dip one slice of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are coated. Allow any excess egg mixture to drip off.
  4. Grease the air fryer basket with butter or cooking spray to prevent sticking.
  5. Place the coated bread slice in the air fryer basket in a single layer.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 for the remaining slices of bread.
  7. Air fry the French Toast slices at 375°F (190°C) for about 4-5 minutes on each side or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  8. Check the French Toast during cooking to ensure it doesn't burn, as cooking times may vary depending on the air fryer model and the thickness of the bread.
  9. Once the French Toast slices are cooked, remove them from the air fryer and transfer to a plate.
  10. Serve the French Toast warm with maple syrup, powdered sugar, fresh fruits, or any of your favorite toppings.

Enjoy your homemade and perfectly crispy French Toast made in the air fryer! This recipe is easy to customize with different types of bread and toppings, making it a delightful breakfast treat for all.
