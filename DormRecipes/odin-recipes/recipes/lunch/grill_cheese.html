Melty Goodness Between Toasted Love!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or your preferred type)
  • 2 slices of your favorite cheese (cheddar, American, Swiss, etc.)
  • Butter or margarine (softened, for spreading)

Steps

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 375째F (190째C) for a few minutes.
  2. Lay out the two slices of bread on a clean surface or plate.
  3. Place one slice of cheese on one of the bread slices.
  4. If you're adding any optional ingredients like ham, turkey, bacon, tomatoes, onions, or pickles, layer them on top of the cheese.
  5. Place the second slice of cheese on top of the optional ingredients (if added).
  6. Top with the second slice of bread to form a sandwich.
  7. Spread a thin layer of softened butter or margarine on the outer sides of the sandwich. This will help the bread toast and become crispy in the air fryer.
  8. Carefully place the prepared grilled cheese sandwich in the air fryer basket.
  9. Air fry the sandwich at 375째F (190째C) for about 4-6 minutes, flipping it over halfway through the cooking time. The cooking time may vary based on the size and thickness of your bread and the power of your air fryer.
  10. The grilled cheese is ready when both sides are golden brown and the cheese inside has melted.
  11. Remove the grilled cheese from the air fryer and let it cool slightly before slicing.
  12. Cut the sandwich diagonally or into halves and serve it warm.

Enjoy your delicious and perfectly crispy grilled cheese sandwich made in the air fryer! The air fryer provides a quick and convenient way to make a classic comfort food, and you can customize the fillings to your liking.
