Savor Plant-Powered Delights!

Ingredients

  • 1 block of firm tofu, pressed and drained
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 small corn tortillas
  • Suggested toppings (shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, avocado slices, salsa, lime wedges, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 375째F (190째C).
  2. Slice the pressed and drained tofu into small rectangular pieces or cubes.
  3. In a mixing bowl, toss the tofu pieces with cornstarch, olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  4. Place the seasoned tofu in the air fryer basket in a single layer.
  5. Air fry the tofu at 375째F (190째C) for about 12-15 minutes, flipping the pieces halfway through the cooking time for even crispness.
  6. While the tofu is air frying, warm the corn tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  7. Once the tofu is crispy and golden brown, remove it from the air fryer.
  8. Assemble your tacos by placing a few pieces of crispy tofu in each tortilla.
  9. Top the tacos with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, avocado slices, salsa, and any other desired toppings.
  10. Serve the Crispy Air Fryer Tofu Tacos with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.

Enjoy your flavorful and protein-packed vegetarian lunch with these tasty tofu tacos!
