Craft Your Perfect Pie!

Ingredients

  • 1 small pizza crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2-3 tablespoons pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (or any cheese of your choice)
  • Your favorite pizza toppings (e.g., pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, etc.)
  • Olive oil or cooking spray (for greasing the air fryer basket)

Steps

  1. Reheat your air fryer to 375째F (190째C) for a few minutes.
  2. Roll out or stretch the pizza crust to your desired thickness. If using a pre-made crust, make sure it fits inside your air fryer basket.
  3. Lightly grease the air fryer basket with olive oil or cooking spray to prevent sticking.
  4. Place the pizza crust in the greased air fryer basket.
  5. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, covering the entire pizza.
  7. Add your favorite pizza toppings on top of the cheese.
  8. Carefully place the air fryer basket with the pizza into the preheated air fryer.
  9. Air fry the pizza at 375째F (190째C) for about 8-10 minutes or until the cheese is melted, bubbly, and slightly golden, and the crust is crispy.
  10. Keep an eye on the pizza during cooking to ensure it doesn't burn, as cooking times may vary based on the air fryer model and thickness of the crust.
  11. Once the pizza is cooked, carefully remove the air fryer basket from the air fryer.
  12. Transfer the pizza to a cutting board and let it cool for a moment.
  13. Slice the pizza into wedges using a pizza cutter or a sharp knife.
  14. Serve your delicious air fryer pizza hot and enjoy!

You can customize this pizza with your favorite sauce, cheese, and toppings to create a pizza that suits your taste preferences. The air fryer will give your pizza a crispy crust and perfectly melted cheese, making it a quick and easy way to enjoy a homemade pizza.
