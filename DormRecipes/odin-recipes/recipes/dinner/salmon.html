Coastal Delights on Your Plate!

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets (skin-on or skinless)
  • Olive oil or melted butter
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: your choice of herbs and spices (such as dill, garlic powder, paprika)

Steps

  1. reheat the Air Fryer: Preheat your air fryer to around 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the Salmon: Pat the salmon fillets dry with a paper towel. This helps to remove excess moisture and ensures a crispy exterior.
  3. Season the Salmon: Brush both sides of the salmon fillets with a thin layer of olive oil or melted butter. Squeeze a bit of lemon juice over the fillets for extra flavor. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and any optional herbs and spices you prefer.
  4. Place in the Air Fryer: Place the seasoned salmon fillets in the air fryer basket, ensuring they are not overlapping. If your air fryer is small, you might need to cook the fillets in batches.
  5. Cook the Salmon: Cook the salmon in the air fryer for about 8-12 minutes, depending on the thickness of the fillets. The general rule of thumb is to cook for about 5-6 minutes per 1/2 inch (1.25 cm) of thickness. Keep an eye on the salmon to avoid overcooking. You can also check the doneness by using a fork to gently flake the salmon, it should easily separate into flakes.
  6. Serve: Once the salmon is cooked to your desired level of doneness, carefully remove it from the air fryer basket. Serve it immediately.
  7. Optional Garnishes: You can garnish your air-fried salmon with additional lemon slices, chopped fresh herbs, or a drizzle of extra olive oil.
  8. Enjoy: Your air-fried salmon is now ready to be enjoyed as a flavorful and nutritious meal!

Remember that cooking times might vary based on the thickness of the salmon fillets and the specific model of your air fryer. It's a good practice to start with a slightly shorter cooking time and then check for doneness before adding more time, as you can always cook it a little longer if needed.
